About Me

Hello, I'm Kyler, a seasoned IT professional with a passion for cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and the limitless potential of cloud technology. With four years of hands-on experience in the dynamic field of IT, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and a strategic mindset to the table.

As an Air Force veteran, I've cultivated a disciplined approach to problem-solving and a commitment to excellence. While my military experience wasn't in IT, it instilled in me the values of teamwork, adaptability, and a strong work ethic—qualities that seamlessly translate into the fast-paced world of information technology.

I thrive on challenges and consider myself a lifelong learner. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity & Information Assurance, I am dedicated to staying at the forefront of industry advancements. Complementing my academic journey, I am actively acquiring certifications in cybersecurity, networking, and cloud computing to enhance my skill set and contribute effectively to the ever-evolving landscape of IT security.

I am on the lookout for a full-time cybersecurity or networking role, ideally in a hybrid capacity in the vibrant Virginia Beach area or in a fully remote capacity. My goal is to contribute my disciplined approach, adaptability, and technical skills to cutting-edge projects that embrace innovation.

Let's connect and discuss how my background and passion for cybersecurity align with your organization's goals.
